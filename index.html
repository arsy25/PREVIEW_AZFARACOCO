Coco Substrate

100% Organic Coconut Sugar

Coconut Sugar from PT Azfara Imany Aroma comes from organic certified soil, the basic ingredients of coconut sugar come from selected coconut tree sap , taken by organic and competent farmers, then processed first by farmers before being sent to the factory, after the palm sap is selected then processed and processed in a modern factory with organic standards, halal procedures, and HACCP procedures.

Geotextile ( Cocomesh ) & Other Coir Products

we leverage the natural coconut coir to produce high-quality coir geotextiles ( Cocomesh ). We supply various kinds of coconut coir derivative products, such as Geotextile (Cocomesh), Door Mat, Pots and custom product

Coconut Charcoal Briquette

With a strong background in this industry, We have established our reputation as a supplier of high quality briquettes in Indonesia with costumization specification and packaging.
